🏆 The Hero Product

Tigrox Tiger Milk King is a 20ml tear-and-drink liquid supplement precision-engineered for kids and adults suffering from chronic cough, stubborn phlegm, and weakened respiratory immunity.

It employs a "Clear, Repair, Nourish" (清、修、养) three-in-one mechanism to dissolve deep-seated phlegm, repair damaged mucosa, and replenish lung energy using advanced liquid extraction technology.

What Makes It Different

⚡ Root-Cause Approach

Unlike OTC cough suppressants that paralyze nerves and trap toxic, bacteria-filled phlegm inside the lungs, Tigrox tackles the root dysfunction — clearing old mucosal garbage and repairing the damaged respiratory lining.

💧 Superior Absorption

Advanced liquid extraction technology ensures absorption rates far surpassing traditional remedies like sweet rock sugar pear soups (which actually generate more phlegm in deficient bodies).

📦 Convenience Factor

20ml tear-and-drink packaging bypasses the execution barrier for busy, exhausted middle-aged professionals who don't have time for complicated brewing or pill regimens.

👥 Target Audience

40+ "exhausted protectors" who are the pillars of their families and workplaces, yet secretly harbor deep anxiety about their failing bodies. Extremely cynical due to years of failed treatments, frustrating antibiotic side effects, and the societal stigma of being the "sick person" everyone avoids.

❤️ Core Desires — Use as Ad Hooks

💡 Strategy Tip
Choose ONE of these core desires and build your entire ad around it. Don't try to cram every benefit into a single video. Hook them with the visceral pain, agitate that specific exhaustion, then seamlessly introduce the "Clear, Repair, Nourish" mechanism.
"I want to stop feeling like a walking biohazard at work."
The social stigma of being the chronic cougher in a modern office is devastating to their middle-aged dignity. Tigrox's targeted phlegm-dissolving mechanism eliminates sudden, loud coughing fits.
"I want to sleep through the night like a baby again."
Being jolted awake by a scratching throat turns nights into torture and days into foggy, unproductive messes. Our formula clears irritants and repairs the mucosa, unplugging the respiratory "alarm system."
"I want to clear this disgusting glue from my throat."
Waking up every morning needing to frantically clear stubborn, sticky phlegm is physically and mentally nauseating. The liquid extraction technology deeply penetrates to dissolve deep-seated old phlegm.
"I want to stop getting sick every time the wind blows."
They feel cursed, catching a cold the moment someone sneezes. Tigrox doesn't just treat the cough — it "nourishes" and replenishes lung energy, rebuilding an immune fortress.
"I want to avoid draining my life savings in the ICU."
Paralyzing dread that ignoring their chronic cough leads to irreversible COPD and financial ruin. A preventative, root-cause solution protects their future wealth and family.
"I want to prove I am not over the hill yet."
They reject the belief that "the machine is just old" and are terrified of becoming useless. Restoring respiratory health proves they're still the strong, reliable family pillar.

🎯 Angles That Win for This Brand

Our most successful angles lean heavily into visceral, highly relatable visual hooks — like the "pain mask" of trying to hold in a cough during a meeting or the misery of a 3 AM coughing fit.

Winning ads immediately invalidate false solutions (like exposing the danger of cough syrups trapping toxins) and pivot to our "Clear, Repair, Nourish" mechanism.

🚫 What NOT to Do
Do not use lazy, generic hooks. This audience is highly cynical. If your ad feels like another cheap "magic pill" or an "IQ tax", they will scroll past instantly. You must lead with empathy for the "exhausted protector" and back it up with a logical, systematic health transformation.
